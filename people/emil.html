Emil is a veteran of the video game industry, starting with the original Playstation. He is an expert in developing and deploying C++ software to challenging platforms, specializing in performance-critical engine architecture and realtime graphics. His open source contributions include three Boost libraries: LEAF, QVM and Exception. When not programming he tinkers with old cars and enjoys road trips, traveling and hiking.
