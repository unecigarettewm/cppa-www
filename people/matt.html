Matt has been working in a variety of engineer and management roles for over 8 years. His contributions to Boost have centered around scientific computing, and he is a maintainer of Boost.Math and Boost.Multiprecision. Matt has a Bachelor’s Degree in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Michigan, a Master’s in Engineering Management from the Missouri University of Science and Technology, and is currently pursuing a second Master's in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. When not programming, he enjoys traveling with his wife Alexandra and powerlifting.
