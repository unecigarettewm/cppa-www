C++ Language Slack Community

This is a multi-channel Slack workspace environment which gathers users of C++ from around the world into the same place so we can all learn from each other. It is like IRC but with modern clients that support multimedia, notifications, and smart devices.

FAQ

  • Why do you need my e-mail?

    Slack is invite based. Your e-mail address is used to register you in the workspace. The invitation tool does not store your address.

  • Who operates this Slack workspace?

    This workspace is paid for and operated in the public interest by The C++ Alliance, Inc. in accordance with the mission to promote the general welfare of the C++ programming language.

  • What are the rules?

    Please remain professional in public channels. People from differing cultures and locales all meet here to discuss C++ topics. We ask that you follow the Code of Conduct.

  • How are the rules enforced?

    Due to the sheer size of the community, it is not practical for admins to police every channel all the time. When you see a user acting poorly, consider letting them know in a nice way. Give a benefit of the doubt generously, as spirited debate is sometimes mistaken for bad behavior.

    If diplomacy fails and disruptions persist, please contact one of the admins privately and explain the nature of the problem.

Code of Conduct

  1. Friendly cooperation

    "First, do no harm."

    Harassment, insults, discrimination, and cyberbullying have no place here.

  2. Safe for work

    Please treat all public channels as Safe For Work (SFW). Don't say anything you wouldn't want a co-worker to see if they pass by your desk.

  3. Stay on-topic

    In every public channel but especially the #learn channel which is the most heavily joined, please refrain from off-topic discussion. In public channels, avoid these topics altogether: politics, religion, race, sexual orientation.

  4. No excessive self-promotion

    A link to your project(s) or news item is of course welcome in the #plug_worthy channel. However keep in mind that this community is not intended to be an advertising platform.

  5. Job offers

    Job offers are welcomed in the #jobs channel, but please include the location, the company, a link to details and keep it in the relevant room.
